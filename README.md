# HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview
This project presents an HR analytics dashboard built in Power BI to monitor workforce composition, hiring trends, attrition, compensation, and employee performance.  
The dashboard is designed for HR managers and executives to quickly assess the current state of the workforce and identify potential risk areas.

The analysis is descriptive and diagnostic.

---

## Objective
Provide a centralized HR overview to answer:
- How many active employees does the organization have?
- What is the attrition rate?
- How competitive are salaries?
- How do performance and satisfaction scores look?
- How is the workforce distributed by gender, age, and department?
- Which recruitment channels are most effective?

---

## Dataset
HR employee-level dataset containing demographic, employment, performance, and recruitment information.

Fields(columns) include:
- Employee ID
- Department
- Job Role
- Gender
- Age
- Hire Date
- Attrition Status
- Salary
- Performance Score
- Satisfaction Score
- Recruitment Source

---

## Key Metrics
- Active Employees
- Attrition Rate
- Average Salary
- Average Performance Score
- Average Employee Satisfaction
- Average Age

---

## Dashboard Components

### KPI Cards
- **Active Employees**: Current workforce size
- **Attrition Rate**: Percentage of employees who left
- **Average Salary**: Mean compensation
- **Average Performance Score**: Rating scale (1–4)
- **Average Satisfaction Score**: Rating scale (1–5)
- **Average Age**: Workforce age profile

## Filters and Interactivity
- Department-level filtering
- Global slicers for role-based views
- Clear filter control for quick reset

All visuals update dynamically based on selected filters.

---

## Insights (Examples)
- Attrition rate is relatively high compared to total headcount.
- Production and IT/IS departments have the largest workforce.
- Most hires come from online job platforms and referrals.
- Workforce is concentrated in the 25–44 age range.
- Gender distribution shows a moderate imbalance.
